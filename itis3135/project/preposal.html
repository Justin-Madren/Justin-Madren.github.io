Purpose of the Website

The purpose of this website is to provide an informative and interactive online presence for the church, helping both members and visitors connect with the church community. The website will:

  • Introduce the church to new visitors, including its mission, beliefs, history, and leadership.
  • Provide information to members, such as service times, ministry programs, and upcoming events.
  • Showcase church activities through photo galleries of events, outreach, and community programs.
  • Encourage participation in ministries, events, and volunteer opportunities.
  • Serve as a communication hub, giving the community easy access to contact information and relevant updates.

This website aims to improve accessibility and engagement by offering an easy-to-navigate, visually appealing, and interactive platform that meets the needs of all users, from regular members to first-time visitors.

Intenend Users

  • Church Members: Regular attendees who want updates, events, and galleries.
  • Potential Visitors / New Guests: People new to the church looking for service times, mission, and programs.
  • Youth & Students: Teenagers and college students interested in youth ministries and interactive content.
  • Donors / Community Supporters: People interested in supporting the church financially or volunteering.
  • General Public / Researchers: Anyone seeking general information about the church or its community involvement.

Overview of Content

  • Church Information: Mission, vision, history, leadership bios, and general information about the church community.
  • Events and Ministries: Details about upcoming services, community events, ministry programs, and volunteer opportunities.
  • Photo Gallery and Media: Interactive gallery showcasing church activities, outreach events, worship services, and community involvement.

Page Design

Page 1: Home

  • Purpose: Introduce the church and provide easy navigation to all other pages.
  • Audience: Visitors, church members, new guests, community members.
  • Content: Welcome message, Photo/banner of the church, Small section: mission or church verse, Quick links to important pages, Service times, Location
  • User Input: None
  • Valadation: None
  • Interaction: Buttons/Links/Drop-downs
  • Actions: Navigation only
  • Notes: This will include the header, footer, and navigation bar.

Page 2: About Us

  • Purpose: Explain the church’s mission, beliefs, leadership team, and history.
  • Audience: New visitors, potential members.
  • Content: Mission statement, History of the church, Pastor/leadership bios, Photos of leadership
  • User Input: No
  • Valadation: None
  • Interaction: Buttons/Links/Drop-downs
  • Actions: Navigation only
  • Notes: Highly informational page about the church.

Page 3: Ministries and Programs

  • Purpose: Show all church ministries (Youth, Children, Worship Team, Outreach, etc.).
  • Audience: Church members, community visitors, parents.
  • Content: List of ministries, Short descriptions, Photos or icons, Meeting times
  • User Input: No
  • Valadation: None
  • Interaction: Buttons/Links/Drop-downs
  • Actions: Navigate to expanded descriptions
  • Notes: This page showcases the church’s involvement.

Page 4: Events

  • Purpose: Display upcoming services, events, gatherings, and outreach activities.
  • Audience: Everyone — members and visitors.
  • Content: Calendar view (simple table or list), Event name, date, time, location, Maybe a flyer-style image
  • User Input: No
  • Valadation: None
  • Interaction: Buttons/Links/Drop-downs
  • Actions: Navigation only
  • Notes: This is a great page for JavaScript interactivity for accordion, tabs, filters, or a simple calendar.

Page 5: Photo Gallery

  • Purpose: Show event photos, ministry photos, and community outreach images.
  • Audience: Members, visitors, families, new guests.
  • Content: Interactive image gallery, Thumbnails, Enlarged image preview (lightbox)
  • User Input: No
  • Valadation: None
  • Interaction: Buttons/Links/Drop-downs
  • Actions: Clicking a photo opens a larger version, Clicking categories filters the gallery
  • Notes: BEST page for JavaScript/jQuery dynamic functionality.
