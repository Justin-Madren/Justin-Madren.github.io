Programs and Ministries

At Lakeview Community Church, our programs and ministries provide opportunities for every member of our church family to grow in faith, build community, and serve others. Whether you’re joining a women’s circle, men’s fellowship, youth group, or one of our outreach and worship committees, each ministry offers a meaningful way to connect with others and share God’s love. From Bible studies and music to community service and fellowship, there’s a place for everyone to belong and make a difference.

Ruth Faucette Circle

Our Mission Statement: To unite women in faith, fellowship, and service, sharing Christ's love by meeting the needs of our church and the community with compassion and purpose.

Women of Faith

The Women of Faith is a diverse group of women from several area churches who meet weekly to study God's Word, fellowship, pray and support each other! This group is open to all women who want to grow in their faith, deepen their knowledge of the Holy Scriptures and make lifelong friends!

Kayo Page Men's Fellowship

Gathering of men for fellowship and basketball. All ages welcome!

Youth Group

Our youth group consists of middle and high school ages. We typically meet on Sunday evenings from 6:00-7:30 and other times when special events are planned. We’d love to have you join us!

Happy Timers

Group of our most seasoned members that meet for food, fellowship, bible study and an osscasional outing. They normally meet on the 2nd Tuesday of the month. All ages welcome, the only requirement is to be happy!

Audio Visual Committee

The AVT (Audio, Visual, Technical) Committee serves the church by providing live sound, projection, streaming and recordings for worship services and rehearsals. We also provide support for special events such as vacation bible school, bible studies, church plays, weddings, funerals, etc. We are a resource for all the technical equipment and systems used by the church.

Evangelism and Worship Committee

The Evangelism and Worship Committee is responsible for evaluating programs designed to encourage new memberships, determining the form and philosophy of worship for the congegration, and researching opportunities for evangelistic outreach and missions. We also oversee the yearly calendar including special services, revivals, church-wide trips, etc.

Adult Choir

The Lakeview Adult Choir includes men and women of all ages who sing praises to the Lord with a variety of Christian music, i.e. Hymns, Southern Gospel, Contemporary, etc. We practice on Sundays at 5:00pm and are always looking for new members!
